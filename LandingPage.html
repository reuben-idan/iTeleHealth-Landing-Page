The Problem

Many individuals in rural Ghana face significant barriers to accessing healthcare. These include long distances to medical facilities, overcrowding at clinics, and a shortage of healthcare professionals. This lack of access leads to delayed diagnoses, worsening health outcomes, and preventable complications, particularly for vulnerable populations like the elderly and those with chronic illnesses.

Our Solution: iTeleHealth

iTeleHealth is a telemedicine application designed to bridge the healthcare gap in rural Ghana. Our platform empowers patients to take control of their health by:

  • Recording symptoms and health data
  • Receiving AI-powered preliminary diagnostic suggestions
  • Connecting with doctors for remote consultations

iTeleHealth works seamlessly even in areas with limited connectivity, ensuring that everyone has access to essential healthcare services.
